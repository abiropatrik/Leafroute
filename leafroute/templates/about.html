About the project

The main goal of Leafroute is the development of an environmentally friendly BI system for logistical companies. This is highly important, since transportation contributes significantly to carbon dioxide emissions. CO₂ is the most harmful material released into the atmosphere, in terms of global warming.

Therefore, the goal of the system is to enable logistical companies to significantly reduce the amount of carbon dioxide generated by transportation. The point of this solution is to ensure data-driven decision making, so that employees from these companies can make responsible decisions in the short term and long term, also from an environmentally fri endly perspective.

The system has two main components: the web application used by employees and a data ware house for data storage. The source database can be administrated from the web interface, and the data goes through an ETL process. On the web interface, users will be able to plan and track transportation processes, manage warehousing operations, and analyze the created dashboards.

Generally transport organizers must choose what type of vehicle, route, and timeframe would they like to use for given delivery. Therefore, when planning a new shipment, the system offers several options for implementation depending on the company’s capabilities, emphasizing their environmental impact. The carbon dioxide emissions related with each route are determined based on internationally accepted standards and the analysis of relevant research.
